Little Vampire

Little Vampire is a light puzzle game where you play as a vampire trying to escape a tomb using light reflections. This was made for the Noorderchallenge2021, a 2 week-long game jam held by my college, Noorderpoort. I was one of the programmers and worked on the light reflection, health and damage and the movement for the bat. I was also in charge of the level design.
